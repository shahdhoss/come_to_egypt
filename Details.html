Egyptian Museum

The Egyptian Museum in Cairo is a treasure trove of ancient history, showcasing over 120,000 artifacts spanning 5,000 years.

Location: 📍 Cairo, Egypt

Activities: 🏺 Explore ancient Egyptian artifacts, visit the Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx.

Opening Hours: ⏰ 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM daily.

Feedback: 🌟 Rated 4.5/5. Visitors love the vast collection of ancient relics and the proximity to the pyramids.

Rixos Sharm El-Sheikh

A paradise for beach lovers and divers with clear waters and coral reefs.

Location: 📍 Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt

Activities: 🏖️ Scuba diving, snorkeling, beach activities, and desert excursions.

Opening Hours: ⏰ Open 24/7 for most resorts and beaches.

Feedback: 🌟 Rated 4.7/5. Perfect for beach lovers and divers, with beautiful coral reefs and clear waters.

Karnak Temple

Explore some of the most famous ancient Egyptian monuments, including the Valley of the Kings and Karnak Temple.

Location: 📍 Luxor, Egypt

Activities: 🏛️ Explore Karnak Temple, Valley of the Kings, and the Temple of Hatshepsut.

Opening Hours: ⏰ 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM daily.

Feedback: 🌟 Rated 4.8/5. Ideal for history buffs and those interested in ancient Egyptian culture.

Great Pyramids

The Great Pyramids of Giza, ancient wonders of the world, stand as timeless symbols of Egypt’s rich history and architectural genius. Built over 4,500 years ago

Location: 📍 Giza, Egypt

Activities: 🏜️ Pyramid tours, camel rides, and cultural exploration.

Opening Hours: ⏰ Open daily from 8 AM to 5 PM.

Feedback: 🌟 Rated 4.8/5. A must-see for history enthusiasts, offering a glimpse into ancient Egyptian civilization and engineering marvels.

Old Market

A vibrant cultural hub where you can experience local life, shop for unique souvenirs, and enjoy authentic Egyptian cuisine.

Location: 📍 Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt

Activities: 🛍️ Shopping, dining, cultural exploration, and sightseeing.

Opening Hours: ⏰ Typically open from 10 AM to 11 PM, but hours may vary.

Feedback: 🌟 Rated 4.5/5. A must-visit for those wanting to experience the local Egyptian charm and vibrant market atmosphere.

Valley of kings

The Valley of the Kings is one of Egypt’s most famous archaeological sites. It is the burial ground of many pharaohs of the New Kingdom, including Tutankhamun, Ramses II, and Seti

Location: 📍 West Bank, Luxor, Egypt

Activities: 🏺 Tomb visits, archaeological exploration, and guided tours.

Opening Hours: ⏰ Typically open from 6 AM to 5 PM.

Feedback: 🌟 Rated 4.8/5. A must-visit for history lovers, offering an awe-inspiring journey into the tombs of ancient Egyptian kings and their legendary treasures.
